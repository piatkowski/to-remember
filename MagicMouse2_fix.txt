Reset NVRAM PRAM (Hold Option+Cmd+P+R on boot)

Unpair Magic Mouse 2 in BT Settings
Remove Magic Mouse 2 in BT Settings

Terminal: sudo pkill bluetoothd

Remove files under /Library/Preferences
com.apple.Bluetooth.plist
com.apple.AppleMultitouchMouse.plist
com.apple.driver.AppleBluetoothMultitouch.mouse.plist
com.apple.driver.AppleHIDMouse.plist

Reboot

Reset MM2:
1. Turn MM2 off
2. Hold mouse button and turn on, hold for 5-10 s
3. Release Mouse button

Open Magic Mouse 2
Disconnect Touch Sensor
Disconnect Battery

Done.
